Festival in the Community

Festival in the Community is our opportunity to screen films in areas of the Springs that might not know about the film festival or to share these fantastic films with those that may not be able to attend. We invite you to come out and support these wonderful and thought-provoking film screenings.

All of these film screenings listed below are free and open to the public.

Sponsored by Veda Salon & Spas.
